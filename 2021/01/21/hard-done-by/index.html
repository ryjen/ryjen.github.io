Hard Done By

I am so “hard done by” (adjective; used, cheated, dejected).

It is like a game of attention, who is the most hard done by?

The winner is also the biggest loser, because they are the most hard done by.

Don’t I get it yet? I am not hard done by. Therefore, I am hard done by.

I once had time, until the more hard done by said I needed to suffer.

I once was unique, until the more hard done by made me a clone.

I once was smart, until the more hard done by gave no quarter.

I once was adaptable, until the bar was raised to stop the idiots from taking over.

I once was sane, until the more hard done by said it was not reality.

I once was relevant, until the more hard done by flipped the tables.

I once was brave, until the more hard done by decided they deserved something in return.

I once had a woman, until the more hard done by decided I lose them.

I once had a friend, until the more hard done by decided I was in bad faith.

I once had a home, until the more hard done by decided the world is fair.

Therefore, I am hard done by. And I win. Nana-nana-boo-boo.
