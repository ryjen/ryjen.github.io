Meatspace (live)

My interests since 2017 have been upgrading my jazz and theory and have not bothered to record with analog.

Songs from my set to record:

  • Cliffy: country acoustic half time version of “Cliffs of Dover” by Eric Johnson.
  • Wicked Third Stone: mashup of “Third Stone from the Sun” by Jimi Hendrix and “Wicked Game” by Chris Isaak.
  • Gertrude Blues: countryish/alternative version of “Traveling Riverside Blues” by Led Zeppelin.
  • Laydya: dark version of Layla by Eric Clapton, also known as the torture song.
  • Classical Beatles: “Hard days night” and “Yesterday” with John Lennon voice melodies.
  • Song for George: cover of the title by Eric Johnson
  • Winter Solstice: cover of the title by the Tea Party
  • Twinkle Blues: alternative of twinkle twinkle little star
  • Plushy: alternative to Plush by Stone Temple Pilots
  • Jingle Jazz: jingle bells alternative
  • Rude Alf: rudolph alternative
  • Blue Boss: blue bossa ‘ish classic

Please kick me in the pants to record more.
