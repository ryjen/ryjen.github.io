Dis Grunt Led

Everything is awesome
   They try to say (to a programmer)
Like some fucking joke
    For getting them laid.
It is their identity
   Better you create it
Than they.

Better they make you obsolete
  With respect
To still be accepted
   In a woman's lay
Like some fucking view
   You can use to chew

Like confidence
  They portray
To put down your pay
  And take the day

To make your anger
  Unfounded and gay
To burn your bridges
  In an indirect way

To make you believe
  And tease the cheese
To laugh at your experience
  And uncomfortable needs

She hates how it is
Like a triumph of selfish rights
Blinding that what does not exist
Like a pimp, or a hairless monkey
With money, like a bitch

They cannot stand
It does not make any sense
That they would go for his cents
Or anything else they must
Make it not matter
For what she needs instead of he

In a feigned love
Of type A stress
Alpha-best and B-lose
Ex-Plaining the ISO late, son (isolation)
Comp-art Mentalize the view
For a version of herself

That cannot be he
The just-ify-ed narcy-cyst
Who cannot get upset
Only upset your back
In kindness

Like mocking settlers
For not partaking
And indulging
In an easy life
Of easy drugs,
Sex and news.
