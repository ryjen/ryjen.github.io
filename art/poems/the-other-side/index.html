The Other Side

The other side of life
  Is Venus 2.0.
Eliminating the bad ones
  To create more heat.

They only like the ones like themselves.
  Only want more
Nothing personal,
  Apparently.

The rest are two-headed inbred morons
  Trying to ruin it
All they can say is
  "We know we are beautiful"

And assume the position
  Of sticking the ass 
You mean
  In your face.
