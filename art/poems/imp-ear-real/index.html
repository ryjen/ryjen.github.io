Imp Ear, Real

The SS Enterprise
  Of the imperialists
Is a catch 22
  Earthship.

They can push you
  To greatness
Or overboard at anytime
  Befriend you so greatly
You will jump
  Of your own will.

Unloyal to my own ship
  Less than a second class citizen
Spoiled to seek safety
  And eat my cake, true.

They say you should be
  Trying to eliminate
As the best defence
  Is an offense
And success only comes
  At a great price.
For which one must learn from, and not turn
  Neither served me well.

My offense is deemed dismissable
  insulting, unworthy of banter or growth
Not serving as a defense
  As well as surrender does.

They imperialists believe
  It comes from the top
And this seems somewhat true.  
  Of a leader or god, or a bald man too. 
The question is, is it good or bad? 
  Or relatively easier if they do.  
Or easier to blame
  When things go ascew.

They must send their bald soldiers
  To do very evil things
Who else would hate life
  As much as they do?
Say the big wigs that dictate,
  What happens to you.
It must be for something they ponder,
  While the victims scream
"take it off or we will find your scalp for you".

Followed by a century
  Of the worst self genocides
The planet has ever known
  Only repressed
Facing total anihilation

Now they all get to be stars
  On media, thanks to the hard work
Of the lesser few.
  Believing its unreal normality
And expected view
  For the others to chew.

So what would come of it?
  A maid protocol too?  
To cleanup bad political fathers,
  bad soldiers of the imps without ears
It's real, future mania too.

A genocide of misinterpreted
  Or misguided reality
Like all the victims were pure
  And beautiful too.  
But who's who?  

Still an option to take
  What good is life
Without love?
  Impossible for anyone too.
If all you hear is "not here"
  And go on without a clue.

It is only a problem
  For one side of humanity
The lesser side
  No discrimination necessary.

Just brutality.
  Some poverty too.
From blame of the other side, 
  Serving as a better you
With less than joy
  In the results like you.
