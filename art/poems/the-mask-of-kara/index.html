The Mask of Kara

Part One:

Paranoid Android 

Gets directed

So no thing is affected

With a hemaroid

Named Floyd

And a bowl of debt 

Full of regret

Is that hope I hear?

Or denial I fear

I love them all

But all never enough

To be the porridge

Of my dreams

The flower turns

It's shame invisible

In a show of pain

For someone else

To mend the shell

Of the lord of flies

It is easy to focus

As they cloak us

On just one word

That is never heard

She only has one idea in mind

You are going to lose

Like you deserve

Because most of the world

Is better than that still

You will get used to it

With a dummy you can't stand

Instead of the death of false

Coming from both

As if somehow the oath

Of rejection

Will make them safe again

Safe again

Without men

From the fear of loss

Or being without a toss

Or their connection

To their fiction

Of the strands of time

That cost a dime

Part Two:

Look for John

without bucks

Son

For the Mask of Kara

Is in your eye

And you have no one

Left to help

It became clear

as it has always been

year after year

There is no win

with an empty friend

only more sin

and no way to end

Some losses are too great

For society to create

Much horror

Of an easy adorer

But what will become

Of the total sum

After the sadness

Does not turn to gladness

When she says farewell

After a lengthy affair

Loud will sound the bell

For your fair share

It matches your guests

In a suit that earns you

The greatest of jests

In such an evil rue

To burn your tree

Into the ground

Ignored is your plea

Joy is never found

Part Three:

It is not me

He cried internally

To keep the flow

Of letting go

Which was only a sign

To the keepers of know

He was not benign

and could not mow

If only he had fought

It might have bought

His ascension

Into another dimension

At first, it seemed

To be too mean

And confusing

The misusing losing


Which only brought

Nothing he sought

Into a lost decade

His soul decayed

They still live

In their ignorant fun

His mind will sour

To their attempts at power
