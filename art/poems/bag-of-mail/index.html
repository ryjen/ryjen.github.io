Bag of Mail, Fee Mail

Women are competitive socially but not physically
   Yet their narcissism is body based

Men are competitive with their bodies but not socially
   Yet their narcissism is based on social inclusion (in the women's world)

Men are supposed to be all equal (religious pacifier for violence?) 
   but we are obviously not (just like women).  
Perhaps diversity, inclusivity and equality amongst our own sex
   matters more than across other orientations.
Quite concievable to be unacceptable
   and this is the gaping hole in society
   causing intollerance in less common parts.

What if the mythological ego identity created by men
  To be accepted by women
  creates an epic divisor of violent proportions
  stemming from losing your narcissism
  or having to protect it.

In summary, pointing to the power structure of women
  starting to abandon any sense of righteous social inclusion
  of men in their world as a reflection of them.   
  Thus perhaps a symptom of men's own inequality amongst themselves
  with the good, the bad, and the blad.
