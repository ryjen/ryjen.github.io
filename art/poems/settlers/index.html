Settlers

Some settle without reason
  Just cause
It's what others do
   It's for the best
Just like the rest

Some settle with a reason
   In hard times
To survive
   With underlying
Managed discontent

Some don't settle unconsciously
   Not knowing their motives
An undefined need
   A traumatic symptom
To pretend they are normal

Some don't settle consciously
   With enough social clout
And basic fulfillment
   Knowing the reasons
Experienced in their killing joke

Could never be it's just for the money
  And lie like it's love
As much as I want to
  Order someone around
Like ownership
  And fullfilling mental roles
Even if it is wanted
  Or exploited just the same too

Love is the killing joke for me it seems
   No woman or man is to me
No child or god either.
   Always is a sword
 Ready to cut through me
   The wound gets raw
 In age you see

So the theory of failure goes
  He cared too much
In craptonics true
  Baby theory 101
Gotta give them something
  To be loved with
Or face the boot
  I'd rather chew
Than you
